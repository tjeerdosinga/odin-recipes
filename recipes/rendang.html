Pizza Hawaii

Ingredients

  • 1 Anis
  • 1 litre coconut milk
  • 6 small onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 5 lomboks
  • 3cm ginger
  • 2cm laos
  • 3cm koenjit
  • 1kg beef

How to make it

  1. Cook anis in coconut milk
  2. Cut everything
  3. mix it to goo in kitchen machine
  4. heat a pan and put all stuff in it
  5. Add meat
  6. Cook for 4 hours
  7. Enjoy!
