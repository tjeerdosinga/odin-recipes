Pizza Hawaii

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pineapple
  • Ham
  • Sauce
  • Basil
  • Cheese

How to make it

  1. Pre-heat oven to 220 Degree Celsius
  2. Roll out premade dough
  3. Cover in sauce
  4. decorate with ham and pineapple
  5. Cover with a lot of cheese
  6. add basil
  7. Place pizza in oven for 20 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
